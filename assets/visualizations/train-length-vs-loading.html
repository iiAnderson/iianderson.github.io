Key Finding: Southeastern has 5-car trains running at 80.9% average loading (98% peak) while also operating 10-12 car trains at 71-74% loading. This isn't a capacity shortage - it's misallocation. Chiltern's problem is different: 88% of their fleet is 2-4 cars, so they can't reallocate even if they wanted to.
